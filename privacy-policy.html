Privacy Policy for Cito

Last Updated: July 27, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Cito collects only the minimum data necessary to function properly and provide a smooth user experience. This includes:

  • Your Twitch User ID (not your email or password)
  • Interaction data (e.g., contest entries, selections, participation clicks)

We do not collect or store:

  • Your real name, email, address, or other personally identifiable information
  • Payment information
  • Any messages or chat content

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data to:

  • Enable interaction with contests and matches
  • Track user participation for fair results
  • Provide performance and analytics data to streamers (anonymized)

We do not use your data for:

  • Advertising or marketing
  • Selling to third parties

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties. However, anonymized, aggregated usage data may be used internally for analytics or performance improvements.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Cito does not use cookies or third-party trackers. All functionality runs within Twitch’s sandboxed environment.

5. Data Storage and Security

We use Twitch’s secure API and follow best practices for extension development. Any data stored (e.g., scores or participation logs) is kept securely and is not tied to personally identifiable information.

6. Your Rights

As a Twitch user:

  • You can revoke Cito’s permissions anytime via your Twitch settings
  • You can request removal of any stored participation data via email
  • You can stop using Cito at any time, with no penalty

7. Children’s Privacy

Cito is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has interacted with Cito, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. Continued use of Cito implies acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For questions or concerns about your data or this policy, contact us at:
